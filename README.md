# Integrated FP&A Decision Suite

An integrated FP&A analytics suite combining budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, and scenario modeling to support risk-aware financial decision-making.

---

## Business Context
Finance and FP&A teams require forward-looking visibility into revenue, costs, and margins while understanding where deviations from plan introduce financial risk.  
This project simulates a real-world FP&A workflow used to monitor performance, identify variance drivers, and evaluate downside scenarios before management decisions are made.

---

## Scope
- 24-month planning and analysis horizon  
- Budget vs Actual variance analysis  
- Revenue and expense forecasting  
- Scenario stress testing (revenue downside, cost inflation)  
- Executive-style performance and risk dashboards  

---

## Analytical Components
**Planning & Budgeting**
- Monthly revenue and expense budgets
- Assumption-driven growth and cost structure

**Performance Monitoring**
- Actual vs budget variance ($ and %)
- Margin and EBITDA trend analysis

**Scenario Analysis**
- Revenue downside scenario
- Cost inflation stress scenari
